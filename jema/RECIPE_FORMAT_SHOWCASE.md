Chapati (Tanzanian Flatbread)

Chapati
Country: Tanzania
-----

Ingredients:
  * Wheat flour
  * water
  * cooking oil
  * salt

Steps:
  1. Mix flour, water, oil, and salt
  2. Knead into soft dough
  3. Roll into flat circles
  4. Cook on hot pan until golden on both sides

 Isombe (Rwandan Cassava Greens)

-----
Isombe
Country: Rwanda
-----

Ingredients:
  * Cassava leaves
  * onion
  * oil
  * salt

Steps:
  1. Boil cassava leaves until soft
  2. Fry onions and garlic
  3. Add leaves
  4. Simmer and serve
